These are the assembly data types in the '.data' initialized variable sections:

Assembly     keyword      memory

Byte          db	        1-byte	(8 bits)
Word          dw	        2-bytes	(16 bits)
Doubleword    dd	        4-bytes	(32 bits)
Quadword      dq	        8-bytes	(64 bits)


You write the keyword in the .data section for usage
